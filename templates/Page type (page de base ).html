Who made Everpage?

Everpage is made by Mark Thomas Miller (@mayvalemark on Twitter).

Does Everpage provide hosting?

No. We’ll provide you with the HTML and CSS, but it’s up to you to find a web host.

How much does Everpage cost?

Everpage is completely free. You can create, edit, and download as many sites as you want.

What browsers do Everpage sites support?

Everpage sites are supported on all modern browsers. However, outdated browsers like Internet Explorer aren’t supported.
